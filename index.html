Senior User Experience Designer

Location: Verona, Wi

My name is Brandon Biro, a Visual and User Experience designer. I am a graduate of the University of Wisconsin-Madison, have certificates from Madison Area Technical College in Web Design, and the Weinschenk Institute for User Experience Design. I am also studying for my Master's Degree in Visual Design and Communication from Liberty University.

I design digital products, brand identies, and user experiences. I'm an advocate for inclusion and accessibility, embrace data and analytics, and have a strong desire to understand the user. I strive and encourage design growth, best practices, and standards.

My goal is to use my design expertise to craft digital experiences based in storytelling, that spark joy and excitement in users.
